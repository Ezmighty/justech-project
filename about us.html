about us

Justech Integrated Resources Concepts Int'l is an indigenous construction company. The company was registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission, Abuja and was incorporated in the year 2018. The company has in its employment core professionals who are knowledgeable and widely experienced in their primary areas. In spite of these, we place emphasis in multi-disciplinary approach to every project. Thus we are able to ensure thoroughness, timeliness and good project delivery.

Justech Integrated Resources Concepts Int'l have been consistent in the construction industry, thereby bringing new innovation to construction at large in Nigeria, we have delivered both micro and macro projects across the country. Our professional capability is put into every aspect of projects.

At Justech Integrated Resources Concepts Int'l, we see our project execution philosophy as the criteria with which each project is handled. We are set to offer you quality and affordability whilst preserving your taste and standard without compromising our integrity.

Since our existence, we are proud to have commenced and delivered bespoke projects across the country which in turn has made us one of the fastest growing indigenous construction company made up of young, vibrant and innovative team.

Vision

To be a key player in the construction industry by running a core professional and dynamic institution guided by sound principles of high integrity, good corporate governance and best practices as the bedrock of policies and operations.
