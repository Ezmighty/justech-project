The Company

Justech Project Int'l is an indigenous construction company. The company was registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission, Abuja and was incorporated in the year 2018. The company has in its employment core professionals who are knowledgeable and widely experienced in their primary areas. In spite of these, we place emphasis in multi-disciplinary approach to every project. Thus we are able to ensure thoroughness, timeliness and good project delivery.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that projects are completed at a competitive price, maintain a safe working environment and deliver high-quality work within project timelines.

Our Vission

To become a leading construction company that consistently delivers projects to clients satisfaction and on schedule.

Our Core Value

Today, Justech Projects Limited is committed to engaging in the construction of highly competitive and top-notch projects, all focused on ensuring client satisfaction, whether for micro or macro projects. We aim to provide clients with an unforgettable experience in the construction of their projects.
